Before your orientation

Three things to do before we meet.

A little preparation goes a long way — the orientation is much more valuable when you've already tried the app.

01

Download magicplan

Search for magicplan in the App Store or Google Play and install it on your device.

02

Explore with two free projects

Every new account gets two free projects. Try scanning a room at home or the office. Come to the orientation with real questions — they shape the session.

03

Note what you want to learn

Jot down anything that felt confusing or that you'd like to go deeper on. The orientation is built around your team's needs.
